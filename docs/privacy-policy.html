Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10/08/2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how ChargeBridge collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email address
  • Name
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Location data (for finding nearby charging stations)

Vehicle Information

  • Car model, year, color
  • License plate number
  • Charging preferences

Usage Data

  • App usage statistics
  • Charging session history
  • Location of charging stations used

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Find nearby charging stations
  • Track your charging history
  • Improve our app functionality
  • Send important notifications about charging sessions

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely on our servers
  • We use industry-standard encryption
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • Location data is only used to provide charging station services

Data Sharing

  • Charging station providers (to facilitate charging sessions)
  • Service providers who assist in app operations
  • Legal authorities when required by law

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of location tracking
